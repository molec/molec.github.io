About

Aspiring front-end developer with a knack for problem-solving and science. 'molecoder' is a play on the word 'molecular'.

I like to dabble in Python and user-interface design. I have been interested in coding since the Myspace days creating personal webpages and tinkering with HTML/CSS. The desire to further my growth in coding sparked from my experiences using software with workflow integration within the science industry. Although the wet lab days are behind me (or who knows.. I might be back one day!), I have always been interested in software design to improve user experience with different machines ranging from running chromatography to generating reports from experiments and even 3D-printing.

One of my goals is to combine a minimalistic style and simple workflow process that will engage easy user interface to decrease visual clutter and provide straightforward productivity for any service or product.
