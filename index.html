About

Self-learner with a knack for problem-solving and science. 'molecoder' is a play on the word 'molecular'.

I like to dabble in webpage design and have been tinkering with HTML, CSS and JS since I was a teen. I hope to further my growth in front-end development and use my experience running scientific workflow systems to create smooth UX/UI products.

Although the wet lab days are behind me now, I have always been interested in software design to improve user experience with different machines ranging from running chromatography to generating reports from experiments and even 3D-printing.

One of my goals is to combine a minimalistic style and simple workflow process that will engage easy user interface by decreasing visual clutter and providing straightforward productivity for any service or product.
