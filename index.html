About

I like to dabble in webpage design and have been tinkering with HTML, CSS and JS since I was a teen. I hope to further my growth in front-end development and use my experience running scientific workflow systems to create smooth UX/UI products. Since my time working as a chemist in wet-labs, I have always been interested in software design to improve user experience with different machines ranging from running chromatography to generating reports from experiments and even 3D-printing.

One of my goals is to combine a minimalistic style and simple workflow process that will engage easy user interface by decreasing visual clutter and providing straightforward productivity for any service or product. I hope to showcase more of my work and personal style through my portfolio.
